How It All Began

A Passion for
Meaningful Celebration

Tees by Dee was founded with one simple belief: every milestone deserves to be remembered beautifully. Whether it's the joy of graduating after years of hard work, or the beauty of a bride's special day — we wanted to create pieces that carry those memories forever.

What started as a small passion project quickly grew into a beloved brand trusted by hundreds of final year students and brides across Nigeria. Our custom signout shirts have graced graduation ceremonies, and our traditional handfans have added elegance to countless weddings.

Every piece is made with intention — carefully crafted, thoughtfully designed, and delivered with love.

How We've Grown

2022

The Beginning

Tees by Dee started as a small passion project, creating custom tees for a small group of final year friends. The response was overwhelming.

2023

Expanding to Bridal

We introduced our traditional handfan collection for weddings and quickly became a go-to brand for brides seeking something truly unique and elegant.

We hit a major milestone — over 500 satisfied customers across Nigeria. Our reputation for quality and reliability grew through word of mouth.
